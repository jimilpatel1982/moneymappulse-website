Frequently asked questions

Common questions about MoneyMap Pulse, your data and our calculators.

What is MoneyMap Pulse?

MoneyMap Pulse is a personal finance planning app with budgeting, net worth, mortgage and calculator tools.

Is MoneyMap Pulse financial advice?

No. MoneyMap Pulse provides calculators and planning tools only. It does not provide regulated financial, investment, mortgage or tax advice.

Do I need an account?

No. MoneyMap Pulse does not require login or account creation.

Where is my data stored?

Data entered in the app is stored locally on your device.

Does MoneyMap Pulse collect my financial data?

MoneyMap Pulse does not use a backend or account system. User-entered data stays on the device unless the user chooses to share it outside the app.

Is MoneyMap Pulse available on iPhone?

MoneyMap Pulse is currently available on Google Play for Android. An iOS version is planned for a future release.

Which calculators are included?

MoneyMap Pulse includes tools for budgeting, net worth, mortgage payment estimates and portfolio return calculations.

How can I send feedback?

You can contact support through the Contact page.
