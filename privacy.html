Privacy Policy

How MoneyMap Pulse handles your information.

Information we collect

MoneyMap Pulse does not require account creation and does not use a backend server for user accounts or cloud sync. The app is designed so that financial information you enter is kept on your device.

We do not operate a sign-in system, user database or cloud storage for your personal finance data in the current version of the app.

Data stored on your device

Financial information entered into MoneyMap Pulse — such as profile details, budget entries, net worth figures and calculator inputs — is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage.

You can review, edit or delete your data within the app. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.

Data sharing

MoneyMap Pulse does not sell user data. We do not share your financial information with advertisers, data brokers or third-party analytics providers through the app.

If you choose to share information outside the app (for example, by exporting or copying data), that sharing is under your control.

Analytics

MoneyMap Pulse does not currently use analytics or tracking services.
