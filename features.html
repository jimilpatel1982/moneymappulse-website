Features

Simple tools to help you understand and plan your personal finances.

Personal finance overview

MoneyMap Pulse helps you create a clearer picture of your finances by combining simple planning tools with practical calculators. Use your profile summary as a personal finance dashboard to review goals, assumptions and calculator results in one place.

Budget planning

Build a household budget across income and expense categories. See monthly totals and understand how your spending compares to your income — a practical starting point for cash-flow planning.

Net worth tracking

Record assets and liabilities to see your overall net worth. Track liquid and illiquid assets separately to understand what is accessible versus longer-term value.

Mortgage calculator

Estimate monthly mortgage payments and explore planning scenarios based on loan amount, interest rate and term. Results are illustrative estimates for personal planning — not mortgage advice.

Portfolio return calculator

Calculate actual investment returns based on contributions and current value over time. Useful for reviewing how your investments have performed — without promising future gains.

Financial calculators

Access multiple practical financial calculators from one hub. Budget, net worth, mortgage and portfolio return tools are available today, with more planning calculators planned for future releases.

Privacy-first design

No login. No cloud account. No backend. Data is stored locally on your device. You stay in control of the information you enter.

Disclaimer: MoneyMap Pulse provides planning tools and calculators only. It does not provide financial, investment, mortgage, tax or regulated advice.
